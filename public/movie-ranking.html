BEST MOVIES FROM BOLLYWOOD

RANKED BY CRITICS AND VIEWERS

3 EDIOTS

3 Idiots is a popular Indian film about three engineering students—Rancho, Farhan, and Raju—who challenge the rigid, competitive academic system at their college. The story follows their journey of friendship, self-discovery, and learning that true success comes from passion and creativity rather than blind conformity. Through humor and emotion, it critiques rote learning and highlights the importance of thinking differently and pursuing one’s dreams.

BAAHUBALI SERIES

The Baahubali series is an epic Indian film saga set in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati. It follows the story of two generations: Amarendra Baahubali, a noble prince beloved by his people, and his son, Mahendra Baahubali, who rises to reclaim his father’s legacy after betrayal by Bhallaladeva, his uncle. The series showcases themes of love, betrayal, bravery, and loyalty, with grand battle sequences and spectacular visuals.

KGF SERIES

KGF (Kolar Gold Fields) is an Indian action drama that follows the rise of Rocky, a young man from poverty who dreams of power and wealth. Set in the 1970s, Rocky infiltrates the dangerous gold mines of KGF, eventually challenging the tyrannical rulers of the mines. Through determination, courage, and ruthless ambition, he becomes a powerful figure, fighting for justice and leaving an unforgettable mark on history.
